About MEMEFI

The perfect fusion of Meme culture and Decentralized Finance

Project Vision

MEMEFI represents the perfect fusion of Meme culture and Decentralized Finance (DeFi), designed to revitalize the currently bearish cryptocurrency market through a powerful economic model, high community consensus, and innovative token mechanisms.

Core Philosophy

Our motto: "Sellers regret, holders earn dividends." MEMEFI transcends the typical Meme coin project, establishing itself as a holder-centric ecosystem that drives value growth through dynamic taxation and follow-up mechanisms.

Holder Supremacy: 95% of follow-up revenue distributed as dividends
Selling Penalty: Selling triggers burning and price increases
Scarcity Driver: Black hole cycle + continuous burning
Community Consensus: Tax revenue funds promotion and development

Follow-up Mechanism

MEMEFI introduces an innovative follow-up selling mechanism, automatically triggered through smart contracts to ensure that selling actions drive price increases rather than decreases.

Trigger Condition

Each time a user sells tokens, the contract automatically calls an equal number of tokens from the black hole to follow the sell.

Fund Distribution

95%: Distributed as weighted dividends in USDT to all token holders, directly deposited into wallets, rewarding long-term holders.

5%: Allocated to the project development fund to support ecosystem growth.
